**Book:** Cancer Medicine

**Full voiceover:**
"A tumor is not a pile of cancer cells. It is an ecosystem. Fibroblasts can reshape the tissue around it. Immune cells can attack, stall, or be recruited into helping. Blood vessels feed the system and shape drug delivery. The extracellular matrix can become a physical and signaling barrier. Inflammation and cell-to-cell signaling change what every cell does next. This is why a drug that kills cancer cells in a dish can fail in a patient. In the body, cancer medicine treats an ecosystem."
