**Book:** Cancer Medicine

**Learning promise:** viewers understand metastasis as a multi-step selection process.

**Hook:** "Metastasis is not travel. It is survival through a gauntlet."

**Full voiceover:**
"Metastasis is not just cancer moving. It is survival through a gauntlet. A cancer cell has to invade surrounding tissue, cross into blood or lymph, survive circulation, exit into a new organ, and then grow in soil it was never meant to live in. Most cells fail. The ones that succeed are not just mobile; they fit the new environment. That is the seed-and-soil idea: the cancer cell is the seed, the organ microenvironment is the soil. Metastasis is why many cancers become lethal, and why treatment has to think like ecology."
