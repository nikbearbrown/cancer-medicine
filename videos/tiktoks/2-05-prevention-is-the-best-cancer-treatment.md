**Full voiceover:**
"The most powerful cancer drug is the one you never need. Cancer prevention is biology plus policy. If tobacco carcinogens damage DNA, reducing exposure prevents mutations. If HPV can drive cancer, vaccination can block the infection that starts the pathway. If ultraviolet radiation mutates skin cells, exposure control changes future risk. Screening matters too, but it is second-best: it finds disease early. Primary prevention keeps some cells from entering the malignant path at all."
